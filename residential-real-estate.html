Residential Real Estate

Expert legal representation for home buyers and sellers throughout New York

Whether you're purchasing your first home, selling a property, or investing in residential real estate, having experienced legal counsel is essential. At Mincone & Mincone, P.C., we guide clients through every aspect of residential real estate transactions, ensuring your interests are protected from contract to closing.

Our Residential Real Estate Services

We provide comprehensive legal services for all types of residential real estate transactions:

  • Title Searches: We conduct thorough title examinations to uncover any liens, encumbrances, or issues that could affect your ownership rights.
  • Contract Review & Negotiation: Our attorneys carefully review purchase agreements, identify potential issues, and negotiate terms that protect your interests.
  • Closing Procedures: We handle all aspects of the closing process, ensuring documents are properly prepared and executed.
  • First-Time Homebuyers: We provide patient guidance to help first-time buyers understand the process and avoid common pitfalls.
  • Investment Properties: For investors, we address the unique legal considerations of purchasing rental or investment properties.

Protecting Your Investment

Buying or selling a home is likely one of the largest financial transactions you'll ever make. Our job is to protect that investment by:

  • Identifying and resolving title issues before they become problems
  • Ensuring all contract terms are fair and properly documented
  • Reviewing survey results and property boundaries
  • Coordinating with lenders, title companies, and other parties
  • Explaining complex legal documents in plain language
  • Representing you at closing to ensure everything proceeds smoothly

Why Choose Us for Your Home Purchase or Sale

With over 33 years of experience in New York real estate law, we've helped countless clients successfully navigate residential transactions. Led by a former CPA, our firm brings a unique financial perspective that helps identify potential issues and opportunities that other attorneys might miss.

We offer same-day consultations and virtual sessions to accommodate your busy schedule. Our responsive team is always available to answer questions and keep you informed throughout the process.
