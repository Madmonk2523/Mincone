About Our Firm

Over three decades of dedication to excellence in real estate law

A Trusted Partner in Real Estate Law

Mincone & Mincone, P.C. is your trusted partner in navigating the complex world of real estate law. With over three decades of experience, we've built a reputation for thorough assessments and innovative solutions.

Our team values long-lasting client relationships, so your interests are always at the forefront of our practice. As a locally-owned firm, we understand the nuances of New York's real estate landscape.

Led by a former CPA, our firm brings a unique financial perspective to your real estate transactions. This combination of legal expertise and financial acumen allows us to identify opportunities and protect our clients from costly mistakes.

Since our establishment in 1992, we have been committed to providing personalized attention to every client, whether you're a first-time homebuyer or a seasoned commercial investor. We take pride in our responsive service and our ability to explain complex legal matters in clear, understandable terms.
