Lender Representation

Comprehensive legal services for financial institutions and private lenders

Financial institutions and private lenders require specialized legal support to protect their interests in real estate lending transactions. At Mincone & Mincone, P.C., we provide comprehensive lender representation services, guiding our clients through all phases of the lending process with precision and expertise.

Our Lender Representation Services

We offer full-service legal support for lenders, including:

  • Mortgage Documentation: We prepare, review, and ensure the proper execution of all mortgage documents, notes, and security instruments.
  • Loan Structuring: Our team assists with structuring loan transactions to meet your requirements while ensuring legal compliance.
  • Foreclosure Proceedings: When borrowers default, we guide lenders through the foreclosure process efficiently and in compliance with all applicable laws.
  • Regulatory Compliance: We help lenders navigate the complex web of federal and state lending regulations.
  • Title Review: We conduct thorough title examinations to ensure the lender's security interest is properly protected.
  • Closing Services: We represent lenders at closings, ensuring all documents are properly executed and funds are properly disbursed.

Guidance Through All Lending Phases

We support lenders throughout the entire lending lifecycle:

  • Pre-Closing: Due diligence review, document preparation, title examination, and compliance review.
  • Closing: Closing representation, document execution, fund disbursement, and recording.
  • Post-Closing: Document review, policy procurement, and file maintenance.
  • Default & Workout: Default management, workout negotiations, and forbearance agreements.
  • Foreclosure: Foreclosure proceedings, REO management, and loss mitigation.

Why Lenders Choose Us

With over 33 years of experience and a former CPA at the helm, we understand both the legal and financial aspects of lending transactions. Our team provides:

  • Thorough attention to documentation and compliance
  • Responsive service to meet your closing deadlines
  • Cost-effective solutions that protect your interests
  • Clear communication throughout the process
  • Experience with residential and commercial lending

We work with banks, credit unions, mortgage companies, private lenders, and other financial institutions throughout New York State.
