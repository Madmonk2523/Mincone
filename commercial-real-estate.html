Commercial Real Estate

Strategic legal counsel for complex commercial transactions

Commercial real estate transactions require sophisticated legal guidance to navigate the complex web of regulations, negotiations, and documentation involved. At Mincone & Mincone, P.C., we provide strategic counsel for businesses, investors, and developers engaged in commercial property transactions throughout New York.

Our Commercial Real Estate Services

We handle all aspects of commercial real estate law, including:

  • Complex Transactions: We structure and negotiate purchases, sales, and exchanges of commercial properties of all types and sizes.
  • Zoning Laws: Our team navigates zoning regulations, variance applications, and land use issues to ensure your project can proceed as planned.
  • Environmental Regulations: We address environmental due diligence, compliance issues, and remediation concerns that affect commercial properties.
  • Financing Issues: We work with lenders and borrowers to structure financing arrangements that meet all parties' needs.
  • Leasing: We draft, review, and negotiate commercial lease agreements for landlords and tenants.
  • Acquisitions & Dispositions: We guide clients through the acquisition and sale of commercial properties with comprehensive due diligence.
  • Development Projects: We assist developers with all legal aspects of new construction and redevelopment projects.

Strategic Counsel for Your Business

Commercial real estate decisions have significant financial implications for your business. Our approach combines thorough legal analysis with practical business insight to help you make informed decisions. With a former CPA leading our firm, we bring a unique financial perspective to every transaction.

We work with:

  • Business owners acquiring or selling commercial properties
  • Real estate investors and investment groups
  • Property developers and construction companies
  • Landlords and property management companies
  • Commercial tenants negotiating lease agreements
  • Banks and financial institutions

Why Choose Us for Commercial Real Estate

With over 33 years of experience in New York real estate law, we understand the unique challenges of commercial transactions. We've successfully handled deals ranging from small retail leases to large commercial developments, always with the same commitment to protecting our clients' interests.

Our responsive team provides same-day consultations and virtual sessions to accommodate the demands of the business world. We keep you informed at every stage and work efficiently to meet your deadlines.
