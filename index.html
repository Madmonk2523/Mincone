Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about real estate law and our services.

New York is one of the states that requires attorney involvement in real estate transactions. A real estate attorney protects your interests by reviewing contracts, conducting title searches, identifying potential issues, and ensuring all legal requirements are met. Our experience helps you avoid costly mistakes and ensures a smooth transaction.

The home buying process typically involves making an offer, signing a contract, conducting inspections, securing financing, and closing. Our team guides you through each step, reviewing all documents, negotiating terms, conducting title searches, and representing you at closing to ensure your interests are protected throughout.
